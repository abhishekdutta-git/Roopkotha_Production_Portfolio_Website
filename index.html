Roopkotha Production is a creative film and music production house dedicated to bringing extraordinary stories to life. Born from a deep love for cinema and music, we specialize in creating content that resonates with the soul and leaves a lasting impression.

Our name "Roopkotha" (রূপকথা) — meaning "fairy tale" in Bengali — reflects our philosophy: every project we undertake is a chance to create something magical, something that transcends the ordinary and transports audiences into worlds of wonder, emotion, and unforgettable experiences.

04 — The Visionary

Meet The
Founder

🎬 Director • Music Producer • Composer

Abhishek Kumar Dutta

Founder & Creative Director — Roopkotha Production

Abhishek Kumar Dutta is a passionate filmmaker and music enthusiast whose creative vision breathes life into Roopkotha Production. With a deep-rooted love for storytelling and an ear for compelling musical compositions, Abhishek founded Roopkotha to create a space where art meets excellence.

His unique approach blends cinematic artistry with musical sensibility, creating productions that don't just entertain — they move people. From intimate short films to large-scale music productions, Abhishek brings the same unwavering commitment to quality and emotional authenticity.

"Every story deserves to be told beautifully. At Roopkotha, we don't just produce content — we craft experiences that stay with you long after the credits roll."

— Abhishek Kumar Dutta
